<section id="about">
<h2>👋 About me</h2>

<p>
I am Zhang San (张三), a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at NetSys Group,
Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua University,
under the supervision of <a href="#">Prof. Li Si</a>.
</p>
<p>
I will be graduating soon. My prior research has focused on congestion control,
flow scheduling, and load balancing in large-scale data center and AI cluster networks,
and I am excited to translate these insights into real-world system performance gains.
</p>
</section>
